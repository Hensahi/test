Personal & Professional information

  • Born August 23, 1978 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • Went to Lower Merion High school (Ardmore, Pennsylvania).
  • Jumped from High School to NBA.
  • NBA draft 1996 / Round: 1 / Pick: 13th overall Selected by the Charlotte Hornets.
  • Playing career 1996–2016 all with Los Angeles Lakers.
  • Position Shooting guard.
  • Numbers: 8, 24.

Professional accomplishments

  • 5× NBA champion (2000–2002, 2009, 2010)
  • 2× NBA Finals MVP (2009, 2010)
  • NBA Most Valuable Player in 2008
  • 18× NBA All-Star (1998, 2000–2016)
  • 4× NBA All-Star Game MVP (2002, 2007, 2009, 2011)
  • 11× All-NBA First Team (2002–2004, 2006–2013)
  • 9× NBA All-Defensive First Team (2000, 2003, 2004, 2006–2011)
  • 2× NBA scoring champion (2006, 2007)
  • NBA Slam Dunk Contest champion (1997)
  • Olympic Gold Medallist: 2008, 2012
  • Hall of Fame

Career statistics

  • Points 33,643 (25.0 ppg).
  • Rebounds 7,047 (5.2 rpg).
  • Assists 6,306 (4.7 apg).
